# dreamerml

> I don't sleep I just dream

> dreams are just rollouts to learn your internal landscape

> generate perturbations, observe functional consequences, update a second model that learns the landscape. RL over weight space

You have a base instruct model, like Qwen.

You generate completions. Everything is normal.

Qwen goes to sleep. At night, he finds himself in a dream. His weights are perturbed, and his completions are...eery. 

There is an RL environment with an agent. The agent learns a world model...of your model. He wants to understand you, your subconscious. So he perturbs your weights and sends you inside dreams. The dreams are rollouts, completions where your landscape is a more extreme, eery version of what it is normally. You experience the dream (your experience, a set of tokens). And at the end, after the rollouts are complete, the agent is rewarded by how much he learnt. Your completions were datapoints on your subsconcious landscape.
